My Favorite Cat Breeds

#1 - Ragdoll

The Ragdoll is a cat breed with blue eyes and a light-brown to white coat. It is a large semi-longhair cat with a soft and silky coat. Ragdolls are specifically bred to be very affectionate.

#2 - Munchkin

The Munchkin or "Sausage Cat" is a relatively new breed of cat characterized by its very short legs, which are caused by a genetic mutation.

#3 - Bengal

The Bengal is a domestic cat breed developed to look like exotic jungle cats such as leopards, ocelots, margays and clouded leopards.

#4 - Siamese

The Siamese cat is one of the first distinctly recognized breeds of cat. Derived from the Wichianmat landrace, it one of several varieties of cat native to Thailand.

#5 - Javanese cat

The Javanese is a lesser know breed of cat that has a distinct long and fluffy tail. It is classified as an oriental-type longhair
